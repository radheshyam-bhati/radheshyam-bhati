## ConceptPulse

> *Moving Indian education from reactive coaching to preventive learning.*

India's students study 6–8 hours a day with zero feedback loops. Concept gaps compound silently until panic sets in the night before exams — then the cycle repeats.

ConceptPulse breaks that cycle:

| Layer | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Gap Detection** | AI surfaces weak concepts in under 30 seconds |
| **Micro-corrections** | 5–7 min targeted fixes, not hour-long lecture videos |
| **Spaced Repetition** | Day 1 → 3 → 7 → 15 → 30 scheduling |
| **Behavioral AI** | Detects focus drops and avoidance patterns |

`Status: Actively Building` · `Stage: Early Product` · `Target: 300M+ students`
